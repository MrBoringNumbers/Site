Music is more than just a passion, it's a way of life.

Our mission is to bring the indie guitar­ist com­mun­ity to­geth­er. Sixty­Seven­Radio pro­vides our growing audi­ence with songs they would have never had the chance to enjoy oth­er­wise. By join­ing the label, you can focus more on your craft and let us worry about making sure people get to hear it!

Interested? Here's How To Join.

1. Send an application.

 The first step towards joining SixtySevenRadio is to send the application. We want to hear about your artistry and what you have to offer. You can click the button below to see the application where you’ll be asked a few questions and can link your demo.

2. We'll get back to you with an offer.

 After submitting your application, our team will review it within 30 days. We will contact you with the email you provide in the application to notify you about any offers we may have for you.

3. You decide if you want to join.

 Finally, you decide if you'd like to accept an offer and join the SixtySevenRadio community! Our team is always available via email if you have any questions or concerns.
