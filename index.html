About Us

Sixty­Seven­Radio is an inde­pend­ent record label and inter­net radio sta­tion blend­ing new-­age, easy listen­ing, and instru­men­tal guitar music. We provide a new type of radio listen­ing experi­ence in response to the over­sat­urated lofi hip-­hop scene. With artists and affili­ates around the world, we empower each musician to stay true to their craft while we focus on show­casing this under­appre­ciated genre. We hope to re­vital­ize this type of music as we embark on a journey with all our artists, and of course, our fans.
